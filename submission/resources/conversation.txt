I interviewed a local barber who wanted a simple and professional website for his barbershop called Hairitage BarberShop. 
The main audience are students, professionals, and families looking for stylish and affordable grooming services. 
The barber preferred dark colors such as black and saddlebrown to create a modern and classic barbershop appearance.
The website showcases haircut services, barber styles, gallery images, and contact information so customers can easily learn more about the business and book grooming services.
